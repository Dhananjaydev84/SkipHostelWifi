Privacy Policy for SkipHostelWifi

Last updated: March 03, 2026

SkipHostelWifi ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension handles information.

1. Information Collection

SkipHostelWifi is designed to be a private, local-first utility. We collect the following information:

  • User ID: Used solely for authenticating with your local hostel WiFi portal.

2. Data Storage

All data collected (your User ID and preferred Target IP) is stored locally on your device using the chrome.storage.local API. We do not maintain any central servers for your data.

3. Data Transmission

The extension only transmits your User ID to the local WiFi portal (e.g., Sophos/Cyberoam) configured in the settings. No data is transmitted to us or any third-party analytics services.

4. Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • storage: To save your User ID locally.
  • alarms: To handle background re-authentication (keep-alive).
  • scripting & tabs: To facilitate the login process.
  • host_permissions: Limited to your local network gateway for authentication.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developers via our GitHub repository.
